A Little About Me

Fun fact: my full name is Cortlin but you can call me Cort. I live in the beautiful state of Utah with my amazing wife and four kids. When I'm with them, there is really no where else I'd rather be.

I also love all things design. From a young age I always had a love for building and understanding how things work. That passion propelled me into my design career over 10 years ago when I got my start in marketing — creating brands and mostly print collateral. Over the last several years, though, I've been more focused on digital ‎— designing software, apps and websites. I especially love building and growing startups but am always looking for ways to turn complex problems into elegant and intuitive user experiences.

Aside from that I enjoy spending time in the mountains, learning new things, playing or watching most sports, trying new foods, and making new friends.
