My Time at Numetric

When I first arrived at Numetric our focus on a general BI market required me to solve complex data management and chart building experiences for an ever-growing analytics platform. About 8 months in we pivoted our focus to building analytics solutions for organizations tasked with improving traffic safety. This young startup has given me many opportunities.

I've lead and helped grow the product team and currently lead all design efforts. I've found a lot of gratification being able to work on a product that has such direct impact on the safety and lives of others.
